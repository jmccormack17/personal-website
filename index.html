About

James McCormack is a computer science student at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a strong passion for data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. He is particularly driven to explore not only the theoretical foundations of these fields but also their wide-ranging applications across industries. His work focuses on harnessing their potential to solve problems, uncover insights, and drive innovation. This site showcases his projects, scholarship, and blogs where he delves into the latest advancements and research in these areas.
