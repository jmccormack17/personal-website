About

James McCormack is a computer science student at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a strong passion for data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. He is driven to explore not only the theoretical foundations of these fields but also their applications across domains. His work focuses on utilizing these technologies to solve problems and uncover insights. This site showcases his projects, scholarship, and blogs where he discusses the latest advancements and research in these areas.
